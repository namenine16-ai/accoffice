# AccOffice Vision

AccOffice is a professional accounting office management platform for small and medium-sized firms in Thailand.

## Mission

Provide a single system that helps accounting teams manage customers, monthly workflows, tax deadlines, documents, billing, reporting, and team collaboration.

## Primary focus areas

- Customer relationship management with structured client profiles.
- Monthly accounting workflow tracking with task assignments, deadlines, and completion status.
- Document and tax filing status monitoring.
- Financial reporting and team coordination.
- A clean, accessible UI based on shadcn/ui and Tailwind CSS.

## Long-term goals

- Expand workflow automation and task scheduling for accountancy operations.
- Add role-based access controls and employee accountability.
- Integrate document storage, bank reconciliation, and tax filing summaries.
- Maintain high code quality through reusable components, strong TypeScript types, and consistent UX patterns.
